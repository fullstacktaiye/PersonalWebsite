About UNO

The University of Nebraska Omaha (UNO) is a public research university located in the heart of Omaha, Nebraska's largest city. Founded in 1908, UNO has evolved into a comprehensive metropolitan university dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality education and fostering strong community engagement.
